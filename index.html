Set Up the Contestify Browser Extension

  1. Download and Extract the Extension:

    Click here to download the ZIP file. Extract the contents to a folder on your computer.

  2. Enable Developer Mode in Browser:

    Open Chrome and go to Menu > More tools > Extensions. Toggle the Developer mode switch in the top-right corner.

  3. Load the Unpacked Extension:

    Click the Load unpacked button and select the extracted folder. The `Contestify` extension will be added to Chrome.

  4. Pin the Extension:

    Click the puzzle icon in the top-right corner, find `Contestify`, and click the pin icon to pin it to the toolbar.

  5. Verify the Extension:

    You should see the `Contestify` icon in your Chrome toolbar. Click it to access the extension.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Contestify Extension?

Once installed, the Contestify Extension will automatically aggregate contests from supported platforms and display them in a unified list. You can access this list by clicking the Contestify icon in your browser toolbar.

Can I receive notifications for upcoming contests?

Yes! Contestify can send you notifications for upcoming and ongoing contests. You can customize your notification preferences in the extension settings.

How do I access the leaderboards?

You can access the leaderboards by clicking on the "Leaderboards" tab within the Contestify Extension. This section will show your ranking and performance across different contests and platforms.

How do I subscribe to email updates?

To subscribe to email updates, go to the extension settings and enter your email address in the subscription section. You will receive regular emails with contest reminders and updates.

Is my data secure with Contestify?

Absolutely. Contestify prioritizes your privacy and data security. We do not store your personal information or track your browsing activity beyond the functionality required to deliver contest updates and notifications.

Can I customize the contest notifications?

Yes, you can customize the frequency and type of notifications you receive. Go to the extension settings to adjust your notification preferences according to your needs.

Is Contestify free to use?

Yes, Contestify is free to use. We aim to provide competitive programmers with the best tools to enhance their contest experience.
